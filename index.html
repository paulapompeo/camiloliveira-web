Freelancer Shirt

Inspired by the businessman shirt, the Freelancer Shirt offers a relaxed fit to the staple blue, white, and pink shirts traditionally worn in corporate settings. Designed to be genderless, the Freelancer Shirt adds style to the unstable life of freedom and flexibility.

Made in limited quantities from premium Italian stock fabrics and available in six different colors, the Freelancer Shirt will look great on you when you finally decide to quit your 9-to-5 job, or on those midweek days when you have no work and still do absolutely nothing (and probably feeling guilty about it).

Shirts are oversized fit and genderless
Three sizes: S/M, M/L, L/XL
Pointed collar
Mother-of-pearl buttons
Asymmetric hem
A5 Single chest pocket
Handmade in limited quantities in Italy

SHIPPING AND RETURN

SHIPPING:

Currently, all shipping is handled directly, with options for hand delivery in Milan or mailing within the EU/Internationally. Shipping costs are calculated separately and added to the order.

RETURN:

Given the limited production scale, exchanging for a different size or color may present challenges. Exchanges are only allowed within the same collection, with the original tag, and within a 30-day period from the date of purchase.

Please send an email to mail@camiloliveira.com with subject "Return" and register your return before sending. Return shipping costs will not be refunded.

Take a moment to carefully read the care instructions on the inside label of your shirt. Shirts will not be eligible for return or refund if they have been misused or poorly cared for during washing.
