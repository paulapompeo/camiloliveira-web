I started making shirts because I couldn’t find one I liked. After watching lots of YouTube tutorials, I learned how to make a pattern and sew. The outcome came out well and I decided to expand it and create my own brand.

My mindset is humble and spontaneous and my brand is about collaboration. I learn with every shirt I make and with the many people whom I meet during every step of its production. Every garment I make is hand- made in Italy, produced with end-of-stock fabrics in limited quantities, and once sold out, they will not be reproduced.

SHIPPING AND RETURN

SHIPPING:

Currently, all shipping is handled directly, with options for hand delivery in Milan or mailing within the EU/Internationally. Shipping costs are calculated separately and added to the order.

RETURN:

As all pieces are made in very small scale, having the same product/ color on a different size might be difficult, please take tha in considetarion...

Please send and email to mail@camiloliveira.com with subject "Return" and register your return before sending. Return shipping costs will not be refunded.

Please read the care instructions on the inside label of your shirt attentively. Shirts will not be accepted for return or refund if they have been misused or poorly cared for during washing.
