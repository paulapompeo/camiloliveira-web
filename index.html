After Sex Shirt

Drawing inspiration from the classic men's tailored shirt, the After Sex Shirt is designed to be genderless, featuring an oversized fit suitable for various occasions. Whether it's worn to covering your body after a sweaty encounter, or simply dressing a sensorial memory.

Shirts are made from premium Italian stock fabrics and available in two sizes in seven different colors and materials.

Every After Sex Shirt comes with a brief, sensuous story written by Francesca Verga.

SHIPPING AND RETURN

SHIPPING:

Currently, all shipping is handled directly, with options for hand delivery in Milan or mailing within the EU/Internationally. Shipping costs are calculated separately and added to the order.

RETURN:

Given the limited production scale, exchanging for a different size or color may present challenges. Exchanges are only allowed within the same collection, with the original tag, and within a 30-day period from the date of purchase.

Please send and email to mail@camiloliveira.com with subject "Return" and register your return before sending. Return shipping costs will not be refunded.

Take a moment to carefully read the care instructions on the inside label of your shirt. Shirts will not be eligible for return or refund if they have been misused or poorly cared for during washing.
